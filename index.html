About Us

At MiniNova Learning Hub, we are dedicated to nurturing young minds through creative, academic, and physical development. Our expert educators and instructors provide a safe, supportive, and fun environment where children can explore their passions and develop essential life skills through Abacus, Bharatnatyam, Yoga, and more.
